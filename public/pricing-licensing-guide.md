## Frequently Asked Questions

### About Licensing

**Q: Can I upgrade from Free to School License mid-year?**
A: Yes! Your data transfers automatically, and we'll prorate the cost.

**Q: What happens if we exceed 50 teachers on a School License?**
A: We'll contact you to upgrade to District License or adjust pricing.

**Q: Can we share a District License across multiple districts?**
A: Each district requires its own license, but we offer volume discounts.

**Q: Do you offer non-profit discounts?**
A: Yes, 501(c)(3) organizations receive 15% off School and District licenses.

### About Implementation

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A: Free tier: immediate. School: 1-2 weeks. District: 4-8 weeks.

**Q: Do we need special equipment?**
A: No, just modern computers/tablets with internet and web browser.

**Q: Is training required?**
A: Not required, but highly recommended for best results.

**Q: Can we pilot before committing district-wide?**
A: Yes! We recommend 2-3 pilot schools for 4-8 weeks.

### About Support

**Q: What if we need help during implementation?**
A: All paid licenses include implementation support via email, chat, or phone.

**Q: Do you offer technical support for students?**
A: Teacher support is included; student support available through teachers.

**Q: What happens if the game doesn't work for us?**
A: 30-day money-back guarantee on all licenses (except Enterprise).

### About Data & Privacy

**Q: Who owns student data?**
A: You do. We never sell or share student data. FERPA/COPPA compliant.

**Q: Can we export our data if we cancel?**
A: Yes, full data export available anytime during active license.

**Q: Where is data stored?**
A: Secure cloud servers (AWS) in the United States. EU hosting available for Enterprise.
