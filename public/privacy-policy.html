Privacy Policy for Nebula Maze

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Onesmo ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Nebula Maze mobile application and web game (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Player Name: Optional username for leaderboard display
  • Game Progress: Level completion, scores, and achievements
  • User Preferences: Settings and game configurations

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and version
  • Usage Data: Game sessions, playtime, and interaction patterns
  • Analytics Data: App performance and crash reports via Firebase Analytics
  • Advertising ID: For personalized ad delivery (Google AdMob)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the game service
  • Saving your game progress and preferences
  • Displaying your scores on leaderboards
  • Improving game performance and user experience
  • Showing relevant advertisements
  • Analytics and bug tracking
  • Processing in-app purchases (via RevenueCat)

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Analytics, authentication, and cloud storage
  • Data Collected: Device information, usage statistics, crash reports
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

4.2 Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Advertising and monetization
  • Data Collected: Advertising ID, device information, ad interactions
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

4.3 RevenueCat

  • Purpose: In-app purchase management and subscription tracking
  • Data Collected: Purchase history, subscription status, user ID
  • Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

4.4 CrazyGames (Web Version)

  • Purpose: Web game hosting and advertising
  • Data Collected: Gameplay data, ad interactions
  • Privacy Policy: CrazyGames Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Game progress is stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Optional cloud save via Firebase (requires sign-in)
  • Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • Encryption: Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL

6. Children's Privacy

Nebula Maze is designed for educational purposes and may be used by children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • We use age-appropriate advertising settings
  • Parents can request deletion of their child's data by contacting us
  • We do not require children to disclose more information than necessary to play the game

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-Out: Disable personalized ads in device settings
  • Data Portability: Export your game data

8. Advertising and Analytics Opt-Out

To opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking
  • Web: Use browser privacy settings or ad blockers

9. Data Retention

  • Game progress: Retained until you delete the app or clear app data
  • Analytics data: Retained for up to 14 months (Firebase default)
  • Purchase history: Retained as required by law and platform policies
  • Inactive accounts: May be deleted after 2 years of inactivity

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Compliance with GDPR (European users)
  • Compliance with CCPA (California users)

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • In-app notification for significant changes
