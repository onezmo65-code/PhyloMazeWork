========================================
QUICK START: CREATE CRAZYGAMES IMAGES
========================================

EASIEST METHOD:
Use the HTML generator I created for you!

========================================
STEP 1: OPEN THE GENERATOR
========================================

1. Navigate to:
   C:\Projects\PhyloMazeWork\create-cover-images.html

2. Double-click to open in your browser

========================================
STEP 2: UPLOAD A SCREENSHOT
========================================

1. Click "Choose File"
2. Browse to: C:\Users\Diska\Pictures\Screenshots
3. Select your best gameplay screenshot
4. Click "Open"

Recommended screenshots:
- Screenshot 2025-09-21 084210.png (good maze view)
- Screenshot 2025-09-30 212653.png (clean gameplay)

========================================
STEP 3: GENERATE IMAGES
========================================

1. Click "Generate Landscape Image"
   - Creates 1920x1080 image
   - Preview appears in browser

2. Click "Download Landscape"
   - Saves: nebula-maze-landscape-cover.png

3. Click "Generate Portrait Image"
   - Creates 800x1200 image
   - Preview appears in browser

4. Click "Download Portrait"
   - Saves: nebula-maze-portrait-cover.png

========================================
STEP 4: UPLOAD TO CRAZYGAMES
========================================

1. Go to CrazyGames Developer Portal:
   https://developer.crazygames.com/games/2563ead6-0e02-4f76-9769-3dccabb559be

2. Scroll to "Cover images" section

3. Upload Landscape (1920x1080):
   - Click "Upload" under "Landscape 16:9"
   - Select: nebula-maze-landscape-cover.png
   - Wait for upload confirmation

4. Upload Portrait (800x1200):
   - Click "Upload" under "Portrait 2:3"
   - Select: nebula-maze-portrait-cover.png
   - Wait for upload confirmation

5. Click "Save and continue to last step"
